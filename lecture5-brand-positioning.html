1 · What is Brand Positioning?
1 · 什么是品牌定位？

Occupying a distinctive place in the mind

在消费者心智中占据独特位置

在目标消费者的心智中建立品牌的战略性位置

Brand positioning is the strategic act of defining where a brand lives — not on a shelf, but in the mind of the consumer. A strong position means that consumers remember the brand for specific, relevant attributes, whether functional or emotional.

Crucially, positioning is not the same as brand identity or brand image. It sits precisely at the intersection of the two: it is the bridge that converts internal identity into an external image.

品牌定位是一种战略行为，定义品牌存在的位置——不是货架上，而是在消费者的心智中。强大的定位意味着消费者会因为特定、相关的属性（无论是功能性的还是情感性的）而记住这个品牌。

关键在于，定位不等同于品牌识别或品牌形象。它恰好处于两者的交汇点：它是将内部识别转化为外部形象的桥梁。

Definition (Johansson & Carlson 2015)

定义（Johansson & Carlson 2015）

"Brand Positioning is the act of defining a brand's strategy to occupy a distinctive place in the mind of target consumers."

"品牌定位是界定品牌战略的行为，旨在目标消费者的心智中占据独特位置。"

Brand Identity 品牌识别 Vision · Values · Style · Origin · Competences · Offering 愿景 · 价值观 · 风格 · 起源 · 能力 · 产品
→
Brand Positioning 品牌定位 The strategic bridge 战略桥梁
→
Brand Image 品牌形象 What consumers perceive & feel 消费者的感知与感受
In other words: Brand positioning can be defined as the shaping of a brand's position towards a desired brand image. It is always both internal (strategy) and external (perception).
换句话说：品牌定位可以被定义为将品牌位置塑造成期望的品牌形象的过程。它既是内部的（战略），也是外部的（感知）。

Requirements for Strong Positioning

  • Fits the brand identity — highlights accurate attributes of the brand
  • Is relevant in the market and communication environment
  • Is relevant to (potential) customers
  • Enables differentiation from the competition
  • Can be pursued in the long term
Ultimate goal: A dominance position in the minds of consumers and maximum differentiation potential compared to relevant competitors.

强定位的要求

  • 符合品牌识别——突出品牌的准确属性
  • 与市场和传播环境相关
  • 与（潜在）客户相关
  • 能够与竞争对手形成差异化
  • 可以长期坚持
最终目标：在消费者心智中占据主导地位，与相关竞争对手相比具有最大的差异化潜力。
2 · Positioning Elements
2 · 定位要素

Value Proposition, Statement & Mantra

价值主张、定位陈述与品牌口号

从内部价值到外部沟通的三个层次

Positioning is communicated at three levels of specificity: the full value proposition, the focused positioning statement, and the memorable slogan or mantra.

定位通过三个具体层次进行传达：完整的价值主张、聚焦的定位陈述以及令人难忘的口号或品牌宣言。

① Value Proposition

① 价值主张

A value proposition is a statement that expresses all outcomes ("benefits") a customer experiences from using a branded product or service. Benefits can be both:

  • Functional — results of product or service features (e.g. a car's fuel efficiency)
  • Emotional — positive feelings experienced when engaging with the product (e.g. pride when driving a BMW)

All benefits should explain how the brand makes up for the cost of the product or service — the customer's "pain."

价值主张是一种陈述，表达客户从使用品牌产品或服务中获得的所有结果（"利益"）。利益可以是：

  • 功能性——产品或服务特性带来的结果（例如汽车的燃油效率）
  • 情感性——与产品互动时体验到的积极感受（例如驾驶宝马时的自豪感）

所有利益都应该解释品牌如何弥补产品或服务的成本——客户的"痛点"。

② Positioning Statement

② 定位陈述

The positioning statement captures the desired brand position by drawing on the most relevant aspects of the value proposition. It follows a standard template:

定位陈述通过提取价值主张中最相关的方面来捕捉期望的品牌位置。它遵循一个标准模板：

Standard Template
标准模板

Our brand is positioned towards [target segment] and offers a [product or service] that is superior in terms of [points of distinction relative to the competition].

我们的品牌面向[目标细分市场]，提供在[相对竞争对手的差异化优势]方面具有优越性的[产品或服务]。

BMW Example: "BMW's position is aimed towards the driver who values performance in a luxurious car that is superior to other luxurious cars in terms of handling, driving comfort and acceleration."
宝马案例："宝马的定位面向重视豪华车性能的驾驶者，在操控性、驾乘舒适性和加速性方面优于其他豪华车。"

③ Slogans vs. Mantras

③ 口号与品牌宣言

Positioning statements are frequently further condensed. But there are two distinct condensed forms for different audiences:

定位陈述通常会进一步凝练。但针对不同受众，有两种不同的凝练形式：

Brand品牌 Slogan (for customers)口号（面向客户） Mantra (for employees)宣言（面向员工）
BMW宝马 The ultimate driving machine终极驾驶机器 Superior driving experience卓越驾驶体验
Nike耐克 Just do it.只管去做 Authentic athletic performance真实的运动表现
Disney迪士尼 Where dreams come true梦想成真之地 Fun family entertainment有趣的家庭娱乐
Apple苹果 Think different.不同凡想 Empowering creative people赋能创意人群
Xiaomi小米 Innovation for everyone创新普及大众 Make quality tech accessible让优质科技触手可及
Key insight: While slogans are aimed at customers and communicate the core benefit/promise, mantras remind employees what the brand is about. Nike's "Authentic Athletic Performance" — coined by Scott Bedbury in the late 1980s — kept the brand on track, differentiated it from Reebok, and inspired employees. That's a great mantra.
核心洞察：虽然口号面向客户并传达核心利益/承诺，但品牌宣言提醒员工品牌的本质。耐克的"真实的运动表现"——由Scott Bedbury在20世纪80年代末创造——使品牌保持正轨，与锐步区别开来，并激励了员工。这就是一个优秀的品牌宣言。
3 · Positioning Maps
3 · 定位地图

Making the invisible visible

让无形变有形

将品牌在消费者心智中的位置可视化

A positioning map is a mental map of consumers' minds — a visual tool showing where competing brands are located based on how consumers score them on relevant attributes. It allows managers to see where they stand, where their competitors are, and where white spaces (unoccupied positions) might exist.

Positioning activities begin by identifying the relevant attributes for the product or service category. These attributes may include both functional and emotional characteristics.

定位地图是消费者心智的可视化工具——显示竞争品牌根据消费者对相关属性的评分所处的位置。它让管理者了解自己所处的位置、竞争对手的位置，以及可能存在的空白空间（未被占据的位置）。

定位活动从识别产品或服务类别的相关属性开始。这些属性可以包括功能性和情感性特征。

How to build a positioning map — 5 steps

如何构建定位地图——5个步骤

① Choose competitors选择竞争对手
Identify the market and the relevant competing brands your brand will be compared with.
识别市场以及与您的品牌进行比较的相关竞争品牌。
② Identify attributes识别属性
Through primary or secondary research, gather the relevant attributes, usage occasions and target group descriptions.
通过初级或次级研究，收集相关属性、使用场合和目标群体描述。
③ Score perceptions评分感知
Collect consumer perceptions by asking respondents to score each brand on each attribute (e.g. Likert scales).
通过要求受访者对每个品牌的每个属性进行评分（如李克特量表）来收集消费者感知。
④ Plot the map绘制地图
Place brands and target group clusters in the map according to the averaged scores. Brands close together compete for the same consumers.
根据平均分数将品牌和目标群体集群放置在地图中。相邻的品牌争夺相同的消费者。
⑤ Identify spaces识别空间
Use the map to identify your current position, your ideal position, gaps, and possible repositioning moves.
使用地图识别您当前的位置、理想位置、差距和可能的重新定位举措。

互动案例：咖啡品牌

Three factors when selecting your target position: (1) Company/Brand — How would the brand like to see itself? (2) Target Group — What do customers want today and in the future? (3) Competition — How are competitors currently positioned?
选择目标定位时的三个因素：(1) 公司/品牌——品牌希望如何看待自己？(2) 目标群体——客户今天和未来想要什么？(3) 竞争——竞争对手目前如何定位？

5 · Positioning Strategies
5 · 定位战略

Moving the brand — when and how

移动品牌——何时及如何

品牌动态化与重新定位的战略选项

Brand dynamisation is used to adapt the brand to current developments and changes in the environment. The relevance of these adjustments has increased due to shorter product life cycles, growing competition, and changes in the communications landscape.

The aim of any positioning strategy is to reduce the perceived distance between the target group's ideal position and the current position of the brand.

品牌动态化用于使品牌适应当前的发展和环境变化。由于产品生命周期缩短、竞争加剧以及传播格局变化，这些调整的重要性日益增加。

任何定位战略的目标都是缩小目标群体理想位置与品牌当前位置之间的感知距离。

The Strategic Options

战略选项

① Maintaining the Position ① 维持现有定位

Brand strategy generally corresponds to the ideal position of the target group. Minor marketing adjustments may be made here and there.

品牌战略通常符合目标群体的理想位置。可能会进行一些小的营销调整。

Example:案例： Rolex has maintained its "timeless luxury precision" positioning for decades, making only incremental product updates.劳力士数十年来一直保持其"永恒奢华精准"的定位，仅进行渐进式产品更新。
② Adaptive Strategy ② 适应性战略

The brand adapts its strategy to changing customer needs — repositioning within the existing space.

品牌将其战略调整以适应不断变化的客户需求——在现有空间内重新定位。

Example:案例： McDonald's went green in 2009, added healthier menu items and pledged sustainable packaging — adapting to health-conscious consumer trends.麦当劳于2009年走向绿色，增加了更健康的菜单项目并承诺使用可持续包装——适应了注重健康的消费趋势。
③ Influencing Strategy ③ 影响性战略

Rather than adapting to customer needs, the brand influences what customers want — shifting the ideal position closer to the brand. Long-term and costly.

品牌不是适应客户需求，而是影响客户想要什么——将理想位置转移到更接近品牌。长期且成本高昂。

Example:案例： Nespresso created an entirely new consumer habit: making premium espresso at home instead of only at cafés.Nespresso创造了一种全新的消费习惯：在家制作高端浓缩咖啡，而不仅仅是在咖啡馆。
④ Extension Strategy ④ 延伸战略

The brand extends its offer profile with a new, relevant characteristic — moving into a new positioning space while keeping the core.

品牌通过新的相关特征扩展其产品配置——在保留核心的同时进入新的定位空间。

Example:案例： Apple: from "Apple Computer Inc." to "Apple Inc." — extended from computers into music, phones, watches, payments.苹果：从"苹果电脑公司"到"苹果公司"——从电脑延伸到音乐、手机、手表、支付。
⑤ New Positioning Strategy ⑤ 新定位战略

The brand's current position is too far from the target group's ideal — major investment is needed to develop a completely new brand image.

品牌当前的位置与目标群体的理想位置相差太远——需要大量投资来建立全新的品牌形象。

Example:案例： Jaguar (2024): went fully electric, dropped the leaping cat, rebranded with an entirely new visual identity targeting a new, younger audience.捷豹（2024年）：完全转型电动化，放弃了跃豹标志，以全新视觉形象重塑品牌，面向更年轻的新受众。
ANTA's multi-brand strategy is a real-world example of the extension + new positioning approach: The core ANTA brand targets mainstream Chinese sports enthusiasts at value prices, while its acquired brands (FILA, Salomon, Arc'teryx, Wilson) cover premium and international segments — all under one corporate umbrella.
安踏的多品牌战略是延伸+新定位方法的现实案例：核心安踏品牌以实惠价格面向中国主流运动爱好者，而其收购的品牌（FILA、萨洛蒙、始祖鸟、威尔逊）覆盖高端和国际细分市场——均在一个企业伞下。
