1 · Consumer Typology
Two ways to look at consumers
看待消费者的两种视角

To identify a consumer typology, there are two fundamentally different approaches. Each answers a different question and leads to different strategic conclusions for brand management.

识别消费者类型有两种根本不同的方法。每种方法回答不同的问题，并为品牌管理提供不同的战略结论。

Approach 1 · Limbic Types
Who is this person — inside?
这个人内心是谁？

Looking from the individual perspective: What emotional drives, personality traits and neurological patterns shape this person's preferences and behaviour? These are stable, biologically rooted, and relatively independent of social class.

从个体视角出发：什么样的情感驱动、人格特征和神经模式塑造了这个人的偏好和行为？这些特征稳定、有生物学根源，相对独立于社会阶层。

Approach 2 · Sinus-Milieus
Where does this person stand — in society?
这个人在社会中处于什么位置？

Looking from the consumer group perspective: Which social milieu, values and life circumstances shape this person's worldview? These shift over generations and vary significantly by region and culture.

从消费群体视角出发：哪种社会阶层、价值观和生活环境塑造了这个人的世界观？这些因素随代际变化而变化，并因地区和文化不同而存在显著差异。

Key insight:核心洞察： Both lenses are needed. A brand that only knows who its customer is (personality) but not where they stand socially will miss important channel, pricing and messaging decisions — and vice versa.
两种视角都不可或缺。一个品牌如果只知道顾客是谁（人格），但不知道他们在社会中处于什么位置，就会错误判断渠道、定价和传播决策——反之亦然。

3 · Limbic Types
Seven emotionally-wired character types
七种情感驱动的人格类型

Limbic® Types is a neuromarketing model by Gruppe Nymphenburg. It categorises people into 7 types based on their dominant emotional drives — rooted in the limbic system of the brain (emotions), not the neocortex (rational thought). The key insight: people are emotionally programmed to prefer certain lifestyles, and these preferences are stable across social conditions.

Limbic®类型是Nymphenburg集团开发的神经营销模型。它根据人们的主导情感驱动将人分为7种类型——植根于大脑的边缘系统（情感），而非新皮质（理性思维）。核心洞察：人们在情感上被"编程"偏好某些生活方式，且这些偏好在不同社会条件下保持稳定。

4 · Sociodemographics
Sociodemographic twins are not identical
社会人口学"双胞胎"并不相同

Sociodemographics (age, gender, income, education) are the traditional way to segment consumers. But they have a fundamental weakness: two people can share identical demographics while having completely different values, lifestyles and brand preferences. The example below became famous in marketing literature.

社会人口学（年龄、性别、收入、教育程度）是传统的消费者细分方式。但它有一个根本缺陷：两个人可以拥有完全相同的人口学特征，却有着截然不同的价值观、生活方式和品牌偏好。以下案例在营销学文献中广为人知。

King Charles III
查尔斯三世国王
Male, born 1948男性，1948年生
Raised in the UK在英国长大

Lives in a castle住在城堡里
Wealthy & famous富有且知名
Ozzy Osbourne
Male, born 1948男性，1948年生
Raised in the UK在英国长大
Married twice结婚两次
Lives in a castle住在城堡里
Wealthy & famous富有且知名
Sociodemographics alone are not enough.
仅靠社会人口学数据远远不够。

King Charles and Ozzy Osbourne share every major demographic variable — yet they represent completely opposite brand worlds. Charles is a Traditionalist (heritage, restraint, duty), while Ozzy is a classic Adventurer (rebellion, excess, anti-establishment). No demographic model would predict this. This is why we need psychographic models like Limbic Types and Sinus-Milieus.

查尔斯国王和奥兹·奥斯本拥有所有主要人口学变量——但他们代表着完全相反的品牌世界。查尔斯是传统主义者（传承、克制、责任），而奥兹是典型的冒险家（反叛、放纵、反建制）。没有任何人口学模型能预测到这一点。这正是我们需要Limbic类型和Sinus阶层这类心理图像学模型的原因。
5 · Sinus-Milieus
Social groups — the Sinus approach
社会群体——Sinus方法

Sinus-Milieus® (Sinus-Institut, Heidelberg) groups people by shared lifeworlds — combining social status (vertical axis) with value orientation (horizontal axis). Their core claim: sociodemographic class and consumer behaviour do not necessarily correlate. The "potato graphic" below shows the 10 German milieus. Click any bubble to explore. Note: we work with the German model as reference; international models exist for 50+ countries.

Sinus-Milieus®（Sinus研究院，海德堡）通过共同生活世界对人群进行分类——将社会地位（纵轴）与价值取向（横轴）相结合。其核心主张：社会人口学阶层与消费行为不一定相关。点击下方的气泡探索各阶层。注：我们以德国模型为参考；国际模型覆盖50余个国家。

6 · Consumer Personas
Build a consumer persona
构建消费者画像

A consumer persona combines both models: a Limbic Type tells you how to talk to a person (emotional tone, messaging), while a Sinus Milieu tells you where to find them and what social context shapes them. Together they create a vivid, actionable picture of a real customer.

消费者画像结合了两种模型：Limbic类型告诉你如何与人交流（情感基调、信息传递），而Sinus阶层告诉你在哪里找到他们以及什么社会环境塑造了他们。两者共同构建一个生动且可操作的真实顾客图像。

Example from lecture:课堂案例： LI Mengya, 20, Beijing, iPhone customer — Sinus: border of Adaptive Navigators / Performers (middle class, modern). Limbic: Performer. → She buys iPhone as a status symbol and identity statement: "I am sophisticated, successful, global."
李梦雅，20岁，北京，iPhone用户——Sinus：适应性导航者/表演者交界处（中产阶级，现代导向）。Limbic：表演者。→ 她购买iPhone作为地位象征和身份表达："我是精致的、成功的、具有全球视野的。"
