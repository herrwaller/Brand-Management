About Germany's Model
关于德国模型

The German Sinus-Milieus® divide society into 10 milieus using the same two axes as the international model: social status (vertical) and value orientation (horizontal: tradition → modernity/reorientation). The model is updated continuously and is the gold standard in German-speaking marketing research.

德国Sinus社会阶层®模型沿相同的两条轴将社会划分为10个阶层：社会地位（纵轴）与价值取向（横轴：传统→现代/重新定向）。该模型持续更新，是德语市场营销研究的权威标准。

DE vs CN — Key contrast
德中对比——核心差异

Germany has a highly differentiated post-material and ecological segment (Postmaterielle + Neo-Ökologische = 20%) absent in China's emerging-market model. China's model features a larger traditional and working-class base. The comparison below makes this visible.

德国拥有高度分化的后物质主义与生态导向阶层（后物质主义者+新生态阶层=20%），这在中国新兴市场模型中几乎没有对应。中国模型的传统阶层与工人阶层基础更为庞大。以下对比直观呈现这一差异。
