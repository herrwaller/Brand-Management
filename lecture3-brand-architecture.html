第一节

Three Basic Brand Types

三种基本品牌类型

Before discussing how to organise multiple brands within a portfolio, it is essential to understand the basic types of brands that exist in the market. Three fundamental brand types can be distinguished by ownership, quality promise, and market positioning.

在讨论如何在品牌组合中组织多个品牌之前，必须先了解市场中存在的基本品牌类型。根据所有权、质量承诺和市场定位，可以区分三种基本品牌类型。

Type 01
National Brands
全国品牌
Manufacturers' Brands / 制造商品牌
Branded products of the producing company, sold under a brand name created, owned and promoted by the manufacturer. These carry the highest quality aspiration and are the most heavily marketed.
生产公司旗下的品牌产品，以制造商创建、拥有并推广的品牌名称出售。这类品牌具有最高的质量追求，也是营销力度最大的。
Nivea · Coca-Cola · Apple
Type 02
Store Brands
自有品牌
Private Label Brands / 私人标签品牌
Products bearing the brand name of the retailer, not the manufacturer. Quality has risen significantly — in some categories matching or exceeding national brands. Available only in the stores of the respective retail chain.
贴有零售商而非制造商品牌名称的产品。近几十年来质量已大幅提升——在某些品类中甚至与全国品牌相当或超过。仅在相应零售连锁店内有售。
Aldi own-brands · REWE Bio · Tesco Finest
Type 03
No-Names
无品牌
Generic Brands / 通用品牌
Unbranded or minimally branded products with low price and fluctuating quality. These attract purely price-sensitive consumers and invest nothing in brand building.
无品牌或弱品牌化的产品，价格低廉，质量参差不齐。这类产品只吸引纯粹价格敏感的消费者，不投入任何品牌建设。
Generic supermarket items

"Pseudo brands are not brands. They are manufacturer's labels. They are 'me-toos' and have poor positioning, poor quality and poor support." — Director of Marketing, Tesco

"伪品牌不是品牌。它们只是制造商的标签——跟风产品，定位模糊、质量低劣、缺乏支撑。" ——乐购营销总监

Section 2
第二节

Luxury Brands

奢侈品牌

Luxury brands occupy a special position in brand management theory and practice. They are not simply premium-priced products — they operate on a fundamentally different logic: desire created through scarcity, heritage, and social aspiration. According to Kapferer (2008), luxury brands exploit a fundamental social mechanism: "desire born of imitating a model" — the desire to emulate the habits of the very wealthy.

The price-perception graph for luxury is the inverse of standard demand curves — higher prices can actually increase desirability. This is the Veblen effect: making a luxury item more expensive signals exclusivity, which in turn heightens its appeal to aspirational consumers.

奢侈品牌在品牌管理理论与实践中占据特殊地位。它们不仅仅是高价产品——它们遵循根本不同的逻辑：通过稀缺性、传承和社会抱负创造渴望。根据Kapferer（2008）的观点，奢侈品牌利用了一种基本社会机制："对标杆的模仿所产生的渴望"——渴望效仿极富阶层的生活方式。

奢侈品的价格-感知曲线与标准需求曲线相反——更高的价格反而可以增加吸引力。这就是凡勃伦效应：将奢侈品定价更高传递出排他性信号，进而提升其对渴望型消费者的吸引力。

🏛️
Heritage & Provenance
传承与来源
A genuine story of origin and craftsmanship. Luxury cannot be invented overnight — it must be earned over generations.
真实的起源故事和工艺传承。奢侈品不能一蹴而就——必须经过几代人的积累。
🔒
Controlled Distribution
受控分销
Luxury avoids mass availability. Where and how a product is sold is as important as the product itself.
奢侈品避免大众化供应。产品在哪里销售以及如何销售与产品本身同等重要。
📐
Anti-Laws of Marketing
反营销法则
Luxury brands can legitimately ignore normal marketing rules — they can be scarce, expensive, and exclusive by design.
奢侈品牌可以合理地忽略正常营销规则——它们可以在设计上做到稀缺、昂贵和排他。
🏆
Aspirational Quality
渴望品质
The luxury brand must be perceived as the best in its category — not just expensive, but genuinely excellent.
奢侈品牌必须被视为其品类中的最佳——不仅仅是昂贵，而是真正卓越。
Section 3
第三节

Brand Architecture Strategies

品牌架构战略

Brand architecture strategy defines both the breadth (which products share a brand name) and depth (how many variations of a brand name exist) of a brand portfolio. It answers three fundamental questions: (1) Which brand names, logos, symbols should we apply to our products? (2) Which different products or services should share the same brand name? (3) How many variations of that brand name should we employ?

Brand architecture serves two core purposes: (a) Clarify brand awareness — improve consumer understanding of the similarities and differences between individual products; and (b) Improve brand image — maximise transfer of brand equity between the corporate brand and individual products.

品牌架构战略定义了品牌组合的广度（哪些产品共用品牌名称）和深度（存在多少品牌名称的变体）。它回答了三个根本性问题：（1）我们应该对产品使用哪些品牌名称、标志和符号？（2）哪些不同的产品或服务应该共用同一品牌名称？（3）我们应该使用多少个品牌名称的变体？

品牌架构有两个核心目的：（a）明确品牌认知——改善消费者对各个产品之间异同的理解；（b）提升品牌形象——最大化企业品牌与各个产品之间的品牌资产转移。

Section 4
第四节

The Four Architecture Strategies

四种架构战略

Strategy 01
Individual Brand Strategy
单一品牌战略
One brand = one product = one promise. Ferrero does this: Nutella, Kinder, Raffaello are each completely independent brands with their own identity, personality and target audience.
一个品牌=一个产品=一个承诺。费列罗采用此策略：Nutella、健达、拉菲尔各自是拥有独立身份、个性和目标受众的完全独立品牌。
+ Clear product profiling + 清晰的产品定位 − Full marketing budget per brand − 每个品牌需要完整营销预算
Strategy 02
Corporate Brand Strategy
企业品牌战略
Branded House
One brand name covers all products. Apple does this: iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch — all carry the same brand. The corporate brand becomes the primary equity vehicle.
一个品牌名称涵盖所有产品。苹果采用此策略：iPhone、iPad、Mac、Apple Watch——均使用同一品牌。企业品牌成为主要的资产载体。
+ Shared marketing budget + 共享营销预算 − Hard to profile individuals − 难以凸显单品特色
Strategy 03
Umbrella Brand Strategy
伞形品牌战略
House of Brands
An umbrella stands for a product group or philosophy. P&G is the archetype: Pampers, Ariel, Gillette are separate brands under P&G's corporate umbrella. Each brand targets different segments.
伞形品牌代表产品群或理念。宝洁是典型：帮宝适、碧浪、吉列是P&G企业伞下的独立品牌。每个品牌针对不同细分市场。
+ Per-product-line philosophy + 每条产品线各有理念 − Risk of brand dilution − 品牌稀释风险
Strategy 04
Endorsed Brand Strategy
背书品牌战略
Two brand names: one endorser, one product brand. Examples: Kellogg's Cornflakes, Nestlé KitKat. The endorser provides credibility; the product brand provides distinctiveness. Combines advantages and risks of both approaches.
两个品牌名称：一个背书者，一个产品品牌。例：家乐氏玉米片、雀巢奇巧。背书者提供可信度；产品品牌提供独特性。兼具两种方法的优势与风险。
+ Best of both worlds + 兼顾两者优势 − Complex brand management − 品牌管理复杂
Section 5
第五节

Strategic Brand Roles

战略品牌角色

Within a brand portfolio, individual brands are not equally important — they play different strategic roles. Understanding these roles prevents cannibalisation, clarifies budget allocation, and ensures the portfolio addresses all relevant market segments.

在品牌组合中，各个品牌的重要性并不相同——它们扮演不同的战略角色。理解这些角色可以防止相互蚕食，明确预算分配，并确保品牌组合覆盖所有相关市场细分。

Case Study Germany · Henkel Detergents

案例 德国 · 汉高洗涤剂

Flagship Brand 旗舰品牌
Persil
Highest quality, premium price, heavy advertising investment. Defines the standard for the category. The brand the company is most proud of and most willing to invest in.
最高质量、溢价定价、大量广告投入。定义品类标准。公司最引以为豪、最愿意投资的品牌。
Flanker / Fighter Brand 侧翼/战斗品牌
Spee
Positioned as "value for money". Its role is to compete with private labels and protect the flagship brand's market share without cannibalising the premium offering.
定位为"性价比"。其作用是与自有品牌竞争，保护旗舰品牌的市场份额，同时不蚕食高端产品。
Cash Cow Brand 现金奶牛品牌
Weisser Riese
Minimal advertising, loyal customer base, high profitability, low growth. Generates margin that funds investment in the flagship and flanker brands.
广告最少、拥有忠实消费群体、利润率高、增长缓慢。产生利润，为旗舰品牌和侧翼品牌的投入提供资金。

Case Study China · Haier Group — Home Appliances

案例 中国 · 海尔集团——家用电器

Flagship Brand 旗舰品牌
卡萨帝 Casarte
Ultra-premium "art of living" appliances — refrigerators, washing machines, and wine cellars priced 3–5× the category average. Launched 2007 to compete with Miele and Sub-Zero in China's high-end segment. Heavy brand investment, flagship store network, design collaborations. Haier's prestige vehicle and margin driver.
超高端"艺术生活"家电——冰箱、洗衣机和酒柜，定价为品类均价的3至5倍。2007年推出，旨在中国高端市场与美诺、Sub-Zero竞争。大量品牌投入、旗舰店网络、设计联名合作。海尔的形象担当与利润引擎。
Flanker / Fighter Brand 侧翼/战斗品牌
统帅 Leader
Online-first, value-for-money sub-brand launched on Tmall and JD.com to compete directly with Xiaomi, Hisense, and TCL in the budget segment. Lean product range, minimal offline presence, aggressive pricing. Shields Haier's core brand from a price war in the mass market.
以线上为主的性价比子品牌，在天猫和京东推出，直接与小米、海信和TCL在预算细分市场竞争。精简产品线，极少线下门店，激进定价。保护海尔核心品牌免受大众市场价格战的冲击。
Cash Cow Brand 现金奶牛品牌
海尔 Haier
The original master brand — globally recognised (no. 1 in white goods by volume, Euromonitor 2023), with deep retailer relationships and a loyal installed base. Growth is moderate, but the brand generates stable, high-volume revenue that cross-subsidises Casarte's premiumisation and Leader's price competition.
原始母品牌——全球知名（欧睿国际2023年白色家电销量第一），与零售商关系深厚，拥有忠实的存量用户群。增速平稳，但品牌创造稳定的高销量收入，交叉补贴卡萨帝的高端化投入和统帅的价格竞争。

Analyse Your Brand's Architecture
分析你的品牌架构

Investigate the brand architecture of the company that owns your chosen brand.

  • (1) Classify your brand: Is it a national brand, store brand, or no-name? Is it a luxury brand?
  • (2) Identify the architecture strategy: Does the parent company use an individual, corporate, umbrella, or endorsed brand strategy — or a hybrid? Justify your answer with examples.
  • (3) Identify the brand's strategic role: Is it a flagship, flanker, or cash cow brand in its portfolio?
  • (4) Visualise the brand hierarchy (2–3 slides).

调查拥有你所选品牌的公司的品牌架构。

  • （1）分类你的品牌：它是全国品牌、自有品牌还是无品牌？是奢侈品牌吗？
  • （2）识别架构战略：母公司使用的是单一品牌、企业品牌、伞形品牌还是背书品牌战略——或者混合策略？用示例证明你的答案。
  • （3）确定品牌的战略角色：在其组合中，它是旗舰品牌、侧翼品牌还是现金奶牛品牌？
  • （4）将品牌层级可视化（2-3页幻灯片）。
