About the Model
关于模型

The Limbic® model (Gruppe Nymphenburg) divides people into seven types based on their limbic personality structures. It is grounded in neuroscience and evolutionary biology and is a leading tool in neuromarketing.

Limbic® 模型（Nymphenburg集团）根据边缘系统人格结构将人们分为七种类型。该模型以脑科学与进化生物学为基础，是神经营销领域的权威工具。

The Two Axes 两条坐标轴

Hedonism ↔ Tradition
享乐主义 ↔ 传统
Vertical axis · 纵轴

This vertical axis spans from pleasure-seeking, stimulation-oriented needs (top) to order-seeking, security-focused attitudes (bottom).

这条纵轴跨越了以寻求愉悦、刺激为导向的需求（上方）与追求秩序、安全为主的态度（下方）。

Openness ↔ Discipline
开放 ↔ 责任
Horizontal axis · 横轴

This horizontal axis spans from openness to novelty, individuality and change (left) to a sense of duty, stability and social responsibility (right).

这条横轴跨越了对新事物的开放性、个性与变化（左侧）与责任感、稳定性及社会责任（右侧）。
