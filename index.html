Suggested workflow 建议学习流程
01
Read the lecture
阅读讲义

Work through the full interactive lecture to get the theoretical foundation, take the Big Five quiz and explore both models in context.

通读完整互动讲义，建立理论基础，完成大五人格测试，在情境中探索两种模型。

02
Explore the models
深入探索各模型

Use the dedicated Limbic Types wheel and the China Sinus map to explore each model in more detail at your own pace.

使用专属的Limbic类型轮盘和中国Sinus地图，按自己的节奏深入了解每个模型。

03
Compare DE & CN
对比德国与中国

Open the German Sinus model and the comparison page to understand how milieu structures differ across societies and what this means for global brands.

打开德国Sinus模型和对比页面，理解阶层结构在不同社会中的差异，以及这对全球品牌意味着什么。

04
Test yourself
检验自己

Play the Brand Game to see if you can correctly apply the Limbic model to real brands. Aim for the adjacent-type cases — that's where the real discussion happens.

通过品牌游戏检验你是否能正确应用Limbic模型。重点关注相邻类型的案例——那里才是真正值得深思的地方。
