Section 1
第一节

Course Journey

课程历程

Over the course of ten lectures, we have built a comprehensive understanding of brand management — from the most foundational questions ("What is a brand?") to the operational and strategic tools that allow brands to grow, adapt, and evolve.

经过十讲，我们建立了对品牌管理的全面理解——从最基础的问题（"什么是品牌？"）到使品牌得以成长、适应和演进的运营与战略工具。

  • Lec 1–3

    Built the conceptual foundation: definitions, the identity/image distinction, and portfolio architecture.

  • Lec 4–5

    Focused on the consumer: psychological typologies (Limbic Types, Big Five), social environments (Sinus-Milieus), and the strategic art of positioning.

  • Lec 6–7

    Addressed the conditions of modern brand management: information overload, benefit structures, and the marketing mix.

  • Lec 8–9

    Covered brand dynamics over time and across borders: internationalisation strategies and evolution paths.

  • Lec 10

    We bring it all together — applying every concept to one real brand in the final project.

  • 第1-3讲

    构建了概念基础：定义、识别/形象区别和组合架构。

  • 第4-5讲

    聚焦于消费者：心理类型学（边缘系统类型、大五人格）、社会环境（Sinus社会阶层）和定位的战略艺术。

  • 第6-7讲

    探讨了现代品牌管理的条件：信息过载、利益结构、营销组合。

  • 第8-9讲

    涵盖了品牌随时间和跨越国界的动态：国际化战略和演进路径。

  • 第10讲

    将一切整合在一起——通过期末项目将每个概念应用于你选择的真实品牌。

Section 2
第二节

Final Assignment Brief

期末作业说明

For the final in-class presentation (Lectures 11 & 12), each student presents a new branded product or service idea for their chosen brand — building directly on the analysis work from earlier assignments. The presentation uses 6 slides, one per step, with 7 minutes to present.

对于期末课堂展示（第11和12讲），每位学生将为其所选品牌提出一个新的品牌产品或服务创意——直接建立在前期作业的分析基础之上。展示使用6页幻灯片，每步一页，展示时间为7分钟。

1

Argumentation Line

论证思路

Based on your SWOT analysis: why does your brand need a new product or service? Which strength do you build on, which opportunity are you seizing, which weakness or threat does the idea address?

基于你的SWOT分析：为什么你的品牌需要新产品或服务？你依托哪项优势，抓住哪个机会，解决哪个劣势或威胁？

2

The New Product / Service

新产品/服务

Describe the new branded product or service. What type of brand extension is it — line extension, category extension, or geographic expansion? Why does this extension make sense for your brand?

描述新的品牌产品或服务。这是哪种类型的品牌延伸——产品线延伸、品类延伸还是地理扩张？为什么这种延伸对你的品牌合理？

3

The USP

独特卖点

What is the Unique Selling Proposition of the new product? What specific benefit — functional or emotional — makes it stand apart from existing alternatives on the market?

新产品的独特卖点是什么？哪种具体利益——功能性或情感性——使其有别于市场上现有的替代品？

4

The (New) Target Group

（新）目标群体

Who is the target consumer for this new product? Describe their profile: demographics, values, lifestyle. Is this the same target group as the parent brand, or a new segment being reached?

这款新产品的目标消费者是谁？描述其画像：人口统计、价值观、生活方式。这与母品牌目标群体相同，还是在触达一个新细分市场？

5

Competitive Positioning

竞争定位

Where does the new product sit relative to its main competitors? Use a positioning map (two relevant axes) to show the competitive space and explain why this position is defensible.

新产品相对于主要竞争对手处于什么位置？使用定位图（两条相关轴线）展示竞争空间，并说明为什么这个位置具有可持续性。

6

Campaign Idea

推广创意

Propose a campaign concept: a slogan, a visual or poster idea, and at least two touchpoint ideas (social media, POS, OOH, influencer, etc.). The campaign must reflect the brand's identity and speak to the new target group.

提出一个推广方案：一句口号、一个视觉或海报创意，以及至少两个触点创意（社交媒体、销售终端、户外广告、网红等）。推广方案须体现品牌个性并针对新目标群体。

Section 3
第三节

Presentation Guidelines

演示指南

Do

  • Be confident — you are the expert on your brand
  • Use a clean layout with consistent visual identity
  • Lead with key insights, not detail
  • Highlight and repeat relevant keywords
  • Use visual examples (product images, ad campaigns)
  • Show new findings — add your own analysis
  • Involve the audience with a question or moment

Avoid

  • Speaking too fast — slow down for key points
  • Reading directly from your slides
  • Standing completely still or moving nervously
  • Ignoring the time limit
  • Rushing through due to nervousness

应该做

  • 自信——你是所选品牌的专家
  • 使用简洁、视觉识别一致的布局
  • 以关键洞察而非细节开头
  • 突出并重复相关关键词
  • 使用视觉示例（产品图片、广告活动）
  • 展示新发现——加入你自己的分析
  • 通过问题或互动时刻吸引观众

应避免

  • 说话太快——在关键点上放慢速度
  • 直接从幻灯片上阅读
  • 完全静止地站立或紧张地走动
  • 忽视时间限制
  • 在紧张中匆忙前进

It is perfectly acceptable to pause, take a breath, and collect your thoughts. Rushing through nervousness makes the content harder to follow.

完全可以暂停、深呼吸并整理思绪。在紧张中匆忙前进会使内容更难理解。

Grading Overview

评分概览

Component Weight Details
Self-Study & Research 50% All assignments submitted throughout the semester. 2 students per lecture present in class (not announced in advance). One short branding quiz.
In-Class Presentation 40% Final 6-slide new product presentation (Lec 11 & 12). Criteria: quality of argumentation, creativity of the product idea, depth of analysis, correct application of course concepts, presentation clarity.
Attendance 10% Active participation across all sessions.
评估组成 权重 详情
自学与研究 50% 整个学期提交的所有作业。每讲2名学生展示最新作业（不事先公布）。一次简短的品牌测验。
课堂展示 40% 期末6页新产品展示（第11和12讲）。评估标准：论证质量、产品创意的创造力、分析深度、课程概念的正确应用、展示清晰度。
出勤率 10% 积极参与所有课程。
Upload requirements: All assignments submitted to the dedicated DingTalk folder. File naming format: StudentNumber_Name_EnglishNickname. All assignments compiled in ONE evolving PPT/PDF document per student.
上传要求：所有作业提交到专用DingTalk文件夹。文件命名格式：学生号_姓名_英文昵称。所有作业每人编辑成一个不断完善的PPT/PDF文档。

"Strategy without execution is just a theory. Your final presentation turns your entire semester of analysis into a concrete idea — a real product, a real audience, and a real campaign. This is what brand management looks like in practice."

"没有执行的战略不过是理论。你的期末展示将整个学期的分析转化为具体创意——一个真实的产品、一个真实的受众和一个真实的推广方案。这就是品牌管理在实践中的样子。"
