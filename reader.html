#1
Introduction — What are Brands?
导论——什么是品牌？

De Pelsmacker, P., Geuens, M. & Van den Bergh, J., 2010. Marketing Communications: A European Perspective, 4th ed. FT/Prentice Hall, pp. 50–56

De Pelsmacker等，2010，《营销传播：欧洲视角》，第4版，FT/Prentice Hall，第50–56页

"You buy a product for what it does. You choose a brand for what it means. A product sits on retailers' shelves. A brand exists in consumers' minds."

「购买产品，是因为它能做什么；选择品牌，是因为它意味着什么。产品陈列在货架上，品牌存活于消费者心中。」

— Batey (2008), cited in De Pelsmacker et al., p. 51

#2
Brand Identity, Image & Personality
品牌识别、形象与个性

Burmann, C., Riley, N.-M., Halaszovich, T.F., Schade, M., 2017. Identity-Based Brand Management. Springer Gabler, Wiesbaden, pp. 42–56

Aaker, J.-L., 1997. Dimensions of Brand Personalities. Journal of Marketing Research, Vol. 34, No. 3, pp. 347–356

Burmann等，2017，《基于身份的品牌管理》，Springer Gabler，第42–56页；Aaker，1997，《品牌个性维度》，《营销研究杂志》，第34卷第3期，第347–356页

"Strong identities are formed through the clear accentuation of specific features in conjunction with a consciousness of skills and past achievements. The same applies to brands."

「强大的身份认同源于对自身特质的清晰彰显，以及对过往成就的深刻自觉。品牌亦然。」

— Burmann et al., Identity-Based Brand Management, 2017, p. 43

#3
Brand Architecture & Portfolio
品牌架构与品牌组合

Burmann, C., Riley, N.-M., Halaszovich, T.F., Schade, M., 2017. Identity-Based Brand Management. Springer Gabler, Wiesbaden, pp. 122–140

Burmann等，2017，《基于身份的品牌管理》，Springer Gabler，第122–140页

"Multi-sensual brand experiences should address as many senses as possible to garner greater attention, recognition and stronger differentiation, which will lead to an increase in the recall effect and in the emotionality of the experience."

「多感官品牌体验应尽可能调动更多感官，以获得更高的关注度、识别度和差异化，进而提升回忆效果与情感深度。」

— Burmann et al., Identity-Based Brand Management, 2017, p. 122

#5
Brand Positioning
品牌定位

Chernev, A., 2015. Strategic Brand Management. Cerebellum Press, Chicago, Illinois, pp. 58–64

Chernev, A.，2015，《战略品牌管理》，Cerebellum Press，芝加哥，第58–64页

"Brand positioning is the process of creating a meaningful and distinct image of a brand in target customers' minds. Brand positioning reflects the set of common benefits that the brand aims to create for all target customers."

「品牌定位是在目标消费者心智中塑造有意义且独特形象的过程。品牌定位体现了品牌旨在为所有目标消费者创造的共同利益集合。」

— Chernev, A., Strategic Brand Management, 2015, p. 58

#6
Market Conditions & Consumer Behaviour
市场条件与消费者行为

Meffert, H., Burmann, C., Koers, M. (Eds.), 2002. Markenmanagement: Grundfragen der identitätsorientierten Markenführung. Gabler Verlag, Wiesbaden (English translation), pp. 32–33

Meffert, Burmann, Koers (主编)，2002，《品牌管理：以身份为导向的品牌经营基础问题》，Gabler Verlag，威斯巴登（英译本），第32–33页

"Consumer motivation encompasses several motives closely related to needs: motivation is directed toward the satisfaction of needs. Consumer behavior can be influenced by satisfying or specifically stimulating certain needs through marketing measures."

「消费者动机由多种与需求密切相关的动机构成：动机指向需求的满足。营销手段通过满足或定向激发特定需求，可有效影响消费者行为。」

— Meffert, Burmann, Koers (Eds.), Markenmanagement, Gabler Verlag, 2002, p. 32

#7
Marketing Mix & Brand Communication
营销组合与品牌传播

Goi, C., 2009. A Review of Marketing Mix. International Journal of Marketing Studies, Vol. 1, No. 1, May 2009

Goi, C.，2009，《营销组合综述》，《国际营销研究杂志》，第1卷第1期，2009年5月

"Kent (1986) refers to the 4Ps of the marketing mix as 'the holy quadruple … of the marketing faith … written in tablets of stone'. Yet the concept remains the dominant framework in both theory and practice."

「Kent（1986）将营销组合的4P称为"营销信仰中的神圣四元组……如同刻在石板上的戒律"。然而时至今日，这一概念仍主导着理论与实践。」

— Goi, C., A Review of Marketing Mix, International Journal of Marketing Studies, Vol. 1, 2009, p. 2

#8
Internationalisation & Global Brands
国际化与全球品牌

Johansson, J.K., Carlson, K.A., 2015. Contemporary Brand Management. SAGE Publications, Los Angeles, pp. 164–189

Johansson, J.K., Carlson, K.A.，2015，《当代品牌管理》，SAGE Publications，洛杉矶，第164–189页

"A global brand isn't a brand which comes from nowhere — it is a brand which may be sold everywhere but it comes from somewhere quite definite."

「全球品牌并非来自虚无——它可以在全球各地销售，但必然源自某个相当明确的地方。」

— Simon Anholt, cited in Johansson & Carlson, Contemporary Brand Management, SAGE, 2015, p. 164

#9
Brand Evolution & Co-Branding
品牌演进与联合品牌

Burmann, C., Riley, N.-M., Halaszovich, T.F., Schade, M., 2017. Identity-Based Brand Management. Springer Gabler, Wiesbaden, pp. 140–165

Burmann等，2017，《基于身份的品牌管理》，Springer Gabler，第140–165页

"Successful brand dynamisation can only succeed if a sufficient number of important identifying features remain unchanged. This 'medium' mix of old, reinforcing information and new characteristics ensures maximum communication impact."

「成功的品牌动态化要求足够多的核心识别特征保持不变。旧有强化信息与新特征的"适度"调和，方能实现最大传播效果。」

— Burmann et al., Identity-Based Brand Management, Springer, 2017, p. 142
