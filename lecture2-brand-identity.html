第一节

Brand Identity vs. Brand Image

品牌识别与品牌形象

At the heart of brand management lies the distinction between what a brand claims to stand for and what people actually believe about it. Brand Identity is the self-image of the brand — it expresses what a brand stands for and should stand for from the perspective of internal target groups (management, employees). It is the intended benefit bundle, the "inside view" crafted by the company.

Brand Image, by contrast, is the result of the individual, subjective perception and decoding of all signals emitted by the brand by external target groups (consumers, media, trade). It is the "outside view" — a firmly anchored, condensed, evaluative image in the minds of consumers. The gap between identity and image is a key tension in brand management. A company may intend a sophisticated, premium identity, but if consumers perceive it as confusing or generic, the brand fails.

品牌管理的核心在于区分品牌宣称代表的是什么，以及人们实际相信的是什么。品牌识别是品牌的自我形象——它从内部目标群体（管理层、员工）的视角表达品牌代表什么、应该代表什么。这是企业精心构建的"内部视角"，即预期的利益组合。

品牌形象则是外部目标群体（消费者、媒体、渠道商）对品牌发出的所有信号进行个人化、主观感知与解码的结果。这是"外部视角"——牢固植根于消费者心智中经过浓缩与评价的品牌印象。识别与形象之间的落差是品牌管理中的核心张力。

第二节

Brand Touchpoints

品牌接触点

Brand touchpoints are every moment a consumer encounters the brand — the mechanisms through which identity is (hopefully) translated into image. Touchpoints span every stage of the customer journey: from advertising and social media to the product itself, its packaging, after-sales service, and even the behaviour of employees. Managing touchpoints consistently is the operational challenge of brand management.

品牌接触点是消费者与品牌的每一次相遇——是将识别（理想情况下）转化为形象的机制。接触点贯穿客户旅程的每个阶段：从广告和社交媒体到产品本身、包装、售后服务，甚至员工行为。一致地管理接触点是品牌管理的运营挑战。

第三节

The Brand Identity Model

品牌身份模型

The most widely used framework for structuring brand identity in this course is the Burmann / Halaszovich / Hemmann Identity Model (2012). Drawing on identity concepts from social sciences and psychology, it proposes that strong human identities emerge from internal reflection of personal strengths, weaknesses, origins, and characteristics. Applied to brands, this yields six core dimensions of brand identity. Each dimension answers a fundamental question about what the brand is and what it stands for.

本课程中用于构建品牌识别框架最广泛使用的是Burmann/Halaszovich/Hemmann身份模型（2012）。该模型借鉴了社会科学和心理学中的身份概念，认为强大的人类身份源于对个人优势、劣势、起源和特征的内部反思。应用于品牌，这产生了品牌识别的六个核心维度。每个维度都回答了关于品牌是什么以及其代表什么的根本性问题。

Section 4
第四节

The Six Dimensions of Brand Identity

品牌识别的六个维度

Dimension 01
Origin
起源
"Where do we come from?"
"我们从哪里来？"
The geographic, cultural, or institutional roots of the brand. Origin creates authenticity and a bond with stakeholders. E.g., Champagne from France, Bratwurst from Nuremberg, Porsche from Stuttgart.
品牌的地理、文化或机构根源。起源创造真实性并与利益相关者建立纽带。例：法国香槟、纽伦堡香肠、斯图加特保时捷。
Dimension 02
Vision
愿景
"Where are we going?"
"我们要去哪里？"
The long-term direction and aspiration. Vision guides strategic decisions and inspires internal teams. E.g., Tesla: "accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy".
品牌的长期方向与抱负。愿景指导战略决策并激励内部团队。例：特斯拉："加速世界向可持续能源的转型"。
Dimension 03
Values
价值观
"What do we believe in?"
"我们相信什么？"
The core ethical convictions and guiding principles. Values shape decision-making and culture. E.g., Patagonia: environmental responsibility above profit.
核心道德信念和指导原则。价值观塑造决策和文化。例：Patagonia：将环境责任置于利润之上。
Dimension 04
Competences
能力
"What can we do?"
"我们擅长什么？"
The unique skills and capabilities that distinguish the brand. These are the "hard" strengths it can credibly claim. E.g., BMW: driving dynamics and engineering precision.
区别品牌的独特技能和能力。这些是品牌能够可信声称的"硬"实力。例：宝马：驾驶动态和工程精度。
Dimension 05
Personality & Style
个性/风格
"How do we communicate?"
"我们如何表达？"
The brand's character and communication style — analogous to human personality. Measured via the Aaker Brand Personality Scale. In practice: tone of voice, visual language, brand character.
品牌的性格和传播风格——类比于人类个性。通过Aaker品牌个性量表衡量。在实践中：语调、视觉语言、品牌性格。
Dimension 06
Benefits
利益
"What do we offer?"
"我们提供什么？"
The functional and emotional benefits the brand promises to deliver. This is the brand's "offer" to consumers — both tangible and intangible.
品牌承诺提供的功能性和情感性利益。这是品牌对消费者的"承诺"——包括有形和无形的。
Section 5
第五节

Brand Personality & the Aaker Scale

品牌个性与Aaker量表

Brand personality refers to the set of human characteristics associated with a brand (Aaker, 1997). Just as we might describe a person as "warm and trustworthy" or "edgy and adventurous", we can describe brands in the same terms. Jennifer Aaker's Brand Personality Scale identifies five core dimensions, each with sub-traits that can be empirically measured in consumer surveys.

In practice, brand personality is often operationalised as communication style — the consistent tone, visual language, and manner of expression across all brand touchpoints. A luxurious brand does not just sell luxury products; it speaks, looks, and behaves in a luxurious way.

品牌个性是指与品牌相关联的一组人格特征（Aaker，1997）。就像我们可以描述一个人"温暖而可信"或"前卫而充满冒险精神"一样，我们也可以用同样的词语描述品牌。Jennifer Aaker的品牌个性量表确定了五个核心维度，每个维度都有可在消费者调查中实证测量的子特征。

在实践中，品牌个性通常被转化为传播"风格"——在所有品牌接触点上一致的语调、视觉语言和表达方式。奢侈品牌不仅仅销售奢侈品；它以奢侈的方式说话、呈现和行事。
